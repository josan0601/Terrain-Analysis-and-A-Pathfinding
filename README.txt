Project: A* pathfinding and Terrain Analysis

Owner Name: Jose Antonio Frias Castillo

Controls:

- Use the buttons on the right to change among the different modes/maps/heuristics/settings
  the values of those settings are seen on the left of the screen.
- Use the buttons on the left to change among the different analysis methods and to turn it on or off.

Executable:

- It shows a demo that includes a player and an enemy that will search for the player using the propagation
mode with the last position the enemy saw the player (it will only move when the mode is set 
to hide and seek and you click once with the right click somewhere in the map) 

Enjoy!
